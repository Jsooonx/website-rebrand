# Aster Studio

Aster Studio is a fictional portfolio for an independent practice that brings
together brand direction, visual identity, and digital experiences.

The project is designed to feel editorial, warm, and precise: quiet in its
overall presentation, but intentional in every detail. Its full brand direction
is documented in [BRANDING.md](BRANDING.md).

## Core message

**Distinct digital identities, made with care.**

## Current experience

- A parallax-led hero and word-by-word entrances for key headlines.
- An approach, selected work, about, services, and pricing experience built
  around the Aster point of view.
- A sticky “In Practice” sequence, stats, testimonials, FAQ, and contact
  footer.
- Motion that becomes simpler for visitors who prefer reduced motion.

## Site structure

| Route | Purpose |
| --- | --- |
